Egreen Store

Egreen Store Pvt Ltd. is an Indian company based in Mangluru, India. Founded by Saurabh shetty and Vishnu in 2020, the company initially focused on groceries, before expanding into other product categories such as farm based products. The service competes primarily with Amazon' s Indian subsidiary, and the domestic rival Snapdeal.Egreen Store held a 39.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry.Egreen is significantly dominant in the sale of apparel, and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of groceries.[8] Egreen also owns a mobile payments service based on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI). U.S.-based retail chain Walmart acquired a 77% controlling stake in Egreen for US$16 billion, valuing it at $22 billion.
