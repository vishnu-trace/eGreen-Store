Egreen Store

The small and medium-sized E-green store is built using MySQL and Django Framwork. Customers always demand for freshly available fruits,vegetables, and grains so here we are with our App which will supply you the Fresh fruits,veggies,and grains.We also resolved farmer problem as this App will benefit more to the Farmer as he don’t have to go anywhere to sell this product ,He can directly upload his product details in this and He can directly sell his product to the Farmer,more profit to the farmer and customer will also get the Groceries at certain desired rate.This the future of E-green Store and Farmer where it will make the farmer work easier.This is very simple to use with this many farmer will benefits and it gives the customer the exact quality and quantity of products they are buying. This project can be further improved by adding tracker system which will exactly tell you the location of your order.

Abstract


This E-green Store has realized the transmission and control of large goods, so as to facilitate the management and decision of Farm based product, and reduce a big burden for Farmer’s and Customer’s. It also can help to improve the work efficiency of Farmer. Its requirements are to provide the basic information maintenance function of customers and products so that farmer can use the function to add, delete, and modify the basic information of products and the customer can use it to add, modify and delete the basic information of Farm based products. E-green Store is very convenient for managing input, output, and searching the products so as to make the work of Farmer efficient and effective.In the aspect of software, the E-green Store system uses Python Djangoframework and Oracle as the background database. Various configurations in computer including input and output capacity, internal memory and external memory capacity are met the requirements of users
